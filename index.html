I'm a Mechanical Engineering major with a Computer Science minor at Columbia University, interested in building hardware and software projects. Most of my time goes into CAD, machining, and hands-on engineering projects. From combat robots to Formula SAE powertrains, paired with a growing interest in humanoid robotics and mechanical design.
